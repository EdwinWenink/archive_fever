About Me

Philosophy

I studied philosophy (bachelor and research master) at the Radboud University, Nijmegen. The philosophical approaches that are dominantly represented in the papers I have written during my research master are deconstruction, hermeneutical philosophy, and phenomenology. Examples of paper topics are 1) critical evaluations of the so-called hermeneutical circularity in the thought of Heidegger and Gadamer 2) the introduction of historicity into Husserl’s phenomenological method in his late thought 3) considerations about the tension between philosophy’s commitment to truth and its use of metaphorical language (both in the work of Heidegger, Derrida and Deleuze) 4) a discussion of the question whether Wittgenstein’s thought on certainty should be understood as being epistemologically foundationalist or non-foundationalist. Besides from those approaches, the research master in Nijmegen includes a two-semester course on a variety of philosophical methods and as well on the writing and oral skills necessary for doing scientific research.


Artificial Intelligence

Currently I am doing a bachelor programme in Artificial Intelligence at the Radboud University. I decided to do a second study because I want to acquire a set of practical skills (programming, mathematics, logic) in a field that is highly interdisciplinary and also interesting from a philosophical point of view.
